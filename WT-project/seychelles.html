Seychelles

Ivory beaches, granite boulders, and turquoise lagoons.

Overview

The Seychelles archipelago offers pristine shores and protected marine life. Island-hop for secluded coves, nature reserves, and Creole culture.

Top Experiences

  • Anse Source d’Argent on La Digue
  • Snorkeling Sainte Anne Marine National Park
  • Vallée de Mai Nature Reserve on Praslin

Best Time to Visit

April–May and October–November for calmer seas and pleasant weather.
