Norway Fjords

Glacier-carved valleys, glassy waters, and dramatic cliffs.

Overview

The Norwegian fjords are among the world’s most stunning natural landscapes. Sail through narrow inlets, ride scenic railways, and hike to panoramic viewpoints.

Top Experiences

  • Boat cruise through Geirangerfjord or Nærøyfjord
  • Hike to Trolltunga or Preikestolen
  • Flåm Railway and Stegastein Viewpoint

Best Time to Visit

Late spring to early autumn for mild weather and accessible trails.
