Lush, Bali

Emerald rice terraces, temples, and hidden waterfalls.

Overview

Bali’s interior is a tapestry of terraces and jungle sanctuaries. Discover serene walks, sunrise viewpoints, and wellness retreats.

Top Experiences

  • Tegallalang Rice Terraces and Campuhan Ridge Walk
  • Tirta Empul Temple and Goa Gajah
  • Hidden waterfalls near Ubud and Munduk

Best Time to Visit

April–October for drier days and clearer views.
